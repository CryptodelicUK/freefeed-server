Pepyatka

Pepyatka is an open source FriendFeed clone. Basically, this is a social real-time feed aggregator that allows you to share cute kitty photos, coordinate upcoming events, dicuss any other cool stuff on the Internet or setup a private Pepyatka instance in your company.
